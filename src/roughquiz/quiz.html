What binyan (verb form) is the Aramaic word 'קְטַל' (q'tal)?
The Aramaic word 'קְטַל' (q'tal) is in the Pe'al form, which is the basic verb form in Aramaic. This is equivalent to the Hebrew 'Qal' binyan. Pe'al is used for simple actions in their active voice.
